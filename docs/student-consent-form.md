## What Is Speakeasy?

Speakeasy is an AI-powered platform that helps you practice and improve public speaking and soft skills. You record presentations, and our AI provides feedback on your delivery, content, and communication.

## Your Participation Is Voluntary

Using Speakeasy is **voluntary**. You may choose not to participate without any penalty or impact on your course grade. Alternative assignments or evaluation methods will be available through your instructor if you opt out.

## What Data We Collect and How It's Used

By consenting, you agree that Speakeasy may collect and process:

- **Video and audio recordings** of your practice sessions
- **Transcripts** of your speech (generated via Deepgram)
- **AI-generated scores and feedback** (generated via AWS Bedrock)
- **Basic account info** (name, university email)

This data is used **exclusively** to provide you with AI-powered feedback. It is not sold, shared for marketing, or used for purposes unrelated to your learning. For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

## Your Right to Withdraw

You may withdraw your consent and stop using Speakeasy **at any time** by contacting us. Upon withdrawal:

- Your recordings, transcripts, and performance data will be deleted
- There will be no academic penalty for withdrawing
- Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

## Data Protection

Your data is encrypted, stored securely on AWS, and handled in alignment with FERPA. Only authorized Speakeasy systems and personnel can access it.
