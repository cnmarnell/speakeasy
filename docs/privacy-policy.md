# Speakeasy — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 8, 2026

## Introduction

Speakeasy ("we," "us," "our") is an AI-powered soft skills and speech learning platform designed for university students. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## What Data We Collect

When you use Speakeasy, we may collect:

- **Account Information** — Name, university email address, course enrollment details
- **Video Recordings** — Recordings of your speech and presentation practice sessions
- **Audio Transcripts** — Text transcriptions generated from your recordings
- **Performance Data** — AI-generated grades, scores, and feedback on your submissions
- **Usage Data** — Login times, feature usage, and basic device/browser information

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power the Speakeasy learning experience:

- **AI Grading & Feedback** — Your recordings are processed through **AWS Bedrock** AI models to generate performance assessments and personalized feedback.
- **Transcription** — Audio from your recordings is sent to **Deepgram** for speech-to-text transcription, which is then used in the grading pipeline.
- **Platform Improvement** — Aggregated, de-identified usage data may be used to improve Speakeasy's features and AI models.

We do **not** use your data for advertising. We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Ever.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Speakeasy:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| AWS Bedrock | AI-powered grading and feedback | Video/audio content, transcripts |
| Deepgram | Speech-to-text transcription | Audio content |

These providers process data on our behalf under contractual obligations to protect your information.

## Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Your data is retained for the duration of your enrollment in a Speakeasy-enabled course, plus 90 days after the course ends.
- **After retention period:** Recordings, transcripts, and performance data are permanently deleted.
- **Early deletion:** You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights below).

## Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure hosted on AWS.

## FERPA Alignment

Speakeasy is designed to comply with the **Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)**. Student records processed through our platform are treated as protected education records. We:

- Act as a "school official" with a legitimate educational interest under FERPA
- Do not disclose education records to third parties without consent, except as permitted by FERPA
- Support institutional compliance with FERPA requirements

## Your Rights

As a Speakeasy user, you have the right to:

- **Access** your data — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** your data — Request corrections to inaccurate information
- **Delete** your data — Request deletion of your recordings, transcripts, and performance data
- **Withdraw** — Stop using Speakeasy at any time; see our Student Consent Form for details

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification. Continued use of Speakeasy after changes constitutes acceptance.
